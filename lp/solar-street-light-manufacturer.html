Frequently Asked Questions

Everything project buyers ask before ordering.

Are you a manufacturer or a trading company?

We are a direct manufacturer with our own factory, giving you factory pricing, quality control, and full OEM/ODM flexibility without middleman markups.

What can be customized under OEM/ODM?

Wattage and battery sizing, optics and beam angle, housing design and color, your logo and branding, retail packaging, and controller firmware. We also develop new models to your spec.

Can you support tenders and large projects?

Yes. We provide certifications (CE, RoHS, ISO 9001) and test reports, lighting design layouts, samples, and staged production to meet tender timelines for 500-5,000+ unit orders.

What are your MOQ, lead time, and warranty?

MOQ is flexible for project buyers. Lead time is typically 15-25 days for custom orders, and all products carry a 3-year warranty with a 25-year solar panel warranty.
