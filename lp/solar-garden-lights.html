Frequently Asked Questions

Everything project buyers ask before ordering.

Are these bright enough for a large park or resort?

Yes. We offer 3W accent lights up to 30W+ area fixtures, and can lay out a mix of bollards, post-top, and pathway lights to meet target lux levels across large landscapes.

Can I choose the color temperature and design?

Absolutely. Warm 3000K, neutral 4000K, and cool 6000K are standard, with custom housing finishes and shapes available for OEM/ODM orders.

How durable are they outdoors?

Housings are die-cast aluminum with PC lenses, IP65 sealed, and UV/corrosion resistant, suitable for coastal and high-humidity environments.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ is flexible for project buyers. Stocked models ship in 7-10 days; custom configurations typically 15-25 days depending on volume.
