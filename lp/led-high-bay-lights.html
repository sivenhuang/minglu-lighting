Frequently Asked Questions

Everything project buyers ask before ordering.

What wattage do I need for my ceiling height?

As a rule of thumb, 100-150W suits 6-9m ceilings and 200-240W suits 9-15m. Send us your area size, ceiling height, and target lux and we will provide a free lighting layout.

How much energy will I save?

Switching from metal-halide or fluorescent to our 150+ lm/W LED high bays typically cuts lighting energy use by 50-70%, with payback often under two years.

Are they suitable for gas stations and workshops?

Yes. IP65 sealing and industrial drivers make them ideal for warehouses, factories, workshops, gas station canopies, and gymnasiums.

What warranty and certifications do you offer?

All high bay fixtures carry a 5-year warranty and CE/RoHS documentation, with additional test reports available for tender projects.
