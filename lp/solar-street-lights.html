Frequently Asked Questions

Everything project buyers ask before ordering.

What is the price of a commercial solar street light?

Pricing depends on wattage, battery capacity, and order volume. As a direct manufacturer we quote factory prices with volume discounts starting at 50 units. Send your pole height, wattage, and destination country for an exact quote within 24 hours.

All-in-one vs split solar street light, which should I choose?

All-in-one (integrated) units install in minutes and suit most roads and pathways. Split systems separate the panel and battery for higher wattage, taller poles, and low-sunlight regions. We help you pick based on your project.

How many rainy days can they run?

Standard configurations deliver 3-5 rainy-day autonomy. For monsoon or high-latitude regions we can size larger batteries and panels for 7+ days.

Do you provide OEM/ODM and certifications?

Yes. We offer custom wattage, branding, and packaging, and supply CE, RoHS, and ISO 9001 documentation. IEC and other test reports are available on request.
