Frequently Asked Questions

Everything project buyers ask before ordering.

How bright is a solar flood light compared to mains?

Our high-lumen models reach up to 40,000 lm, comparable to traditional metal-halide floodlights, while eliminating wiring and power costs entirely.

Will they work through the night?

Yes. Timer and remote dimming (1/3, 2/3, full) let you tune brightness so lights run all night, including several rainy days of autonomy.

Where can I install them?

Wall, pole, or ground mounting, with no trenching or electrician needed, ideal for billboards, yards, courts, farms, and security perimeters.

Do you supply certifications and OEM?

Yes, CE and RoHS documentation is standard, and we provide OEM/ODM custom wattage, branding, and packaging for project and distributor buyers.
