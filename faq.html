Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering solar and LED outdoor lighting from Minglu Lighting — manufacturing, certifications, MOQ, shipping and support.

Company & Trust

Are you a manufacturer or a trading company?
We are a direct manufacturer of solar and LED outdoor lighting, based in Guangdong, China, producing since 2014. With 10+ years of experience, we have shipped 50,000+ units to 30+ countries, so you buy straight from the factory rather than a middleman.
What certifications do your products have?
Our products are CE, RoHS and ISO 9001 certified, with IP65/IP66 waterproof ratings. Test reports are available on request to support your local market compliance.
Can you share references or past projects?
Yes. We have delivered 500+ projects worldwide across municipal, commercial and residential applications. Visit our Projects page, or contact us for case studies relevant to your region.

Ordering & Pricing

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ depends on the product model. Small trial orders are welcome so you can evaluate quality before scaling up. Tell us your target model and quantity and we will confirm the exact terms.
Can I get a sample before a bulk order?
Absolutely. We provide samples so you can verify build quality, brightness and weather resistance before committing to a bulk order.
How do I get a quote?
Send us the model, quantity and destination port via our Contact form, WhatsApp (+86 18098910947) or email (minglulighting@gmail.com). We usually reply with a quote within 24 hours.
What payment terms do you accept?
We support common international methods such as T/T (deposit plus balance against shipment). Exact terms are confirmed per order and can be discussed for long-term partnerships.

Product & Technical

How long do your solar street lights last?
LED components are rated for 50,000+ hours of operation, and solar panels carry a 25-year performance warranty. Battery life typically runs several years depending on the model and climate.
How many hours per night and how many rainy days of backup?
Most models deliver full-night lighting and 3 to 5 days of backup on cloudy or rainy days, depending on battery capacity and your local sunlight conditions.
Are the lights waterproof and built for harsh weather?
Yes. All outdoor units are rated IP65/IP66 and built to withstand rain, dust, heat and coastal or salty air, making them suitable for demanding outdoor environments.
Do you offer OEM/ODM or custom solutions?
Yes. We support custom wattage, color temperature, pole height, battery size, logo and packaging to match your project and brand requirements.

Shipping & After-sales

Do you ship worldwide and what are the options?
Yes, we ship globally by sea and air, and can handle FOB, CIF or door-to-door delivery depending on your preference and order size.
What warranty and after-sales support do you provide?
Products come with a standard warranty (solar panels up to 25 years). We supply spare parts and remote technical support. Contact us with your order details and our team will assist.
How long is production and delivery?
Typical lead time is 2 to 4 weeks depending on order volume and customization, plus international shipping time to your port. We provide a firm schedule together with the quote.

Still have questions?

Our team usually replies within 24 hours. Reach out for a free quote or technical advice.
