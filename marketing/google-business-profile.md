## 7. Verification (the step only you can do)
- Google will offer postcard, phone, SMS, email, or video verification depending on category/region.
- For China manufacturers, **video verification** is often fastest: record the storefront/factory sign, the workspace, and proof of management access.
- Keep the business name on any signage identical to "Minglu Lighting".

---

## 8. Bing Places (do right after GBP)
- Go to https://www.bingplaces.com/ → **Import from Google Business Profile** to copy everything in one step.
- Verify separately (usually phone/email). Same NAP, same description, same images.

## 9. Ongoing (once live)
- Post a GBP "Update" or "Offer" every 1–2 weeks (new product, project, trade show).
- Ask satisfied B2B clients for reviews (even a few lift the panel).
- Keep hours, phone, and website current — any NAP drift weakens all citations.
