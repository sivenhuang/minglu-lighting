Solar Street Light

Our solar street lights combine high-efficiency monocrystalline solar panels, long-life LiFePO4 batteries, and advanced MPPT controllers. Available in all-in-one and split-type configurations from 30W to 300W, these lights are engineered for reliable outdoor illumination with zero electricity cost.
