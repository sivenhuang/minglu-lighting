Who We Are

Minglu Lighting is a professional manufacturer of solar and LED lighting products, based in Zhongshan — the lighting capital of China. With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in designing and producing high-performance outdoor lighting solutions for global B2B clients.

Our product range covers solar street lights, solar garden lights, LED flood lights, high bay lights, and specialized lighting for industrial and commercial applications. Every product is engineered for durability, energy efficiency, and reliable performance in diverse environments.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, energy-efficient lighting solutions that help our clients reduce energy costs and carbon footprint — while delivering exceptional value and service. We believe good lighting should be accessible, sustainable, and built to last.
