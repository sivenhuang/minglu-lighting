Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: July 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Minglu Lighting ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Shenzhen Minglu Lighting Co., Ltd. collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you visit minglulighting.com (the "Site") or contact us through any channel.

2. Information We Collect

  • Inquiry Information: name, email, phone number, company, product category, and message you submit via our contact channels or WhatsApp.
  • Communication Data: conversations and files shared through WhatsApp or email.
  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring URLs via analytics technologies.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: as described in Section 4.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotations
  • Provide technical support and after-sales service
  • Improve our products, Site, and user experience
  • Send relevant product updates (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal and contractual obligations

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use the following technologies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — collects anonymized usage data to understand Site performance.
  • Google Ads — measures advertising performance and conversions.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect Site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We share data only with processors necessary to operate the Site: Google (Analytics / Ads), WhatsApp / Meta, and our email provider. These parties process data under their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and communication data for as long as necessary to serve your project and for legal / compliance purposes (typically up to 5 years), after which it is deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or object to the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at minglulighting@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures (SSL / TLS encryption, access controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their personal data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
