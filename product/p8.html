FAQ

Solar — FAQs

Is the Solar solar light waterproof? What is its IP rating?
Yes. The Solar is rated IP65 impact protection, with a durable aluminum housing and PC lens. It is built to withstand rain, dust, heat and year-round outdoor use.
How long does the Solar battery last and how many rainy days of backup?
It uses a LiFePO4 (lithium iron phosphate) battery with about a 5-year service life. On a full charge it delivers up to 3 rainy or cloudy days of backup lighting, and MPPT charging reaches full in roughly 5 hours of effective sunshine.
How bright is the Solar and can I choose the color temperature?
The Solar powered led street light uses high-efficiency SMD LEDs with a wide beam angle for strong, even illumination. Color temperature is selectable across 3000K–6500K (warm to cool white) to match your project's ambience.
Does the Solar turn on automatically at night?
Yes. It combines a light sensor, PIR motion sensor and time control. It switches on at dusk automatically and dims from 100% to 30% when no motion is detected, saving energy while keeping the area lit.
What certifications does the Solar have and what is the warranty?
The Solar is RoHS, CE, ISO 9001 certified and comes with a 3 years. Solar panels and LEDs are rated for long service life, backed by our technical support.
What mounting height and pole spacing is recommended for the Solar?
Recommended mounting height is 3–8 m with pole spacing of 10–20 m (model-specific). It can be wall- or pole-mounted and requires no wiring.
Can the Solar be customized for my project (OEM/ODM)?
Yes. As a direct manufacturer we support customization of power, color temperature, housing color, logo and packaging, as well as OEM/ODM for larger projects.
What applications is the Solar suitable for?
It is ideal for Street, Road, Outdoor and solar — well suited to residential, commercial and municipal outdoor lighting.
