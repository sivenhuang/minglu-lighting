About the Solar street light

The Solar street light is a commercial-grade solar street light engineered by Minglu Lighting for reliable, energy-efficient outdoor illumination. Built with high-efficiency LED technology and a durable, weather-resistant housing, it delivers consistent performance for municipal, commercial, and residential projects alike. Every unit is CE, RoHS and ISO 9001 certified and supported by a 5-year warranty.

Typical Applications

  • Roadways & highways
  • Parking lots & garages
  • Residential streets & campuses
  • Pathways & bike lanes
  • Public squares & plazas
